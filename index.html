About Leigh

Leigh enjoys smiling, reading, listening, tinkering, and sketching. Sometimes she gets wrapped up in her head, but she always come back (so far). Yoga & Raspberry Pi are Leigh's new addictions. Check out her posts about them.

What do you do?

I never stop learning. Here's some stuff I can do: craft UI/UX (HTML5, SASS, JS/jQuery), design & fulfill brand identity, and code silly browser games. Currently, I'm teaching myself Ember.js by writing a small application that totals Seven Wonders scores. When that's done, I plan on undertaking a more ambitious overhaul of my aborted mobile app: The Village

How do you do it?

I am a natural problem-solver, attentive listener, and I always trust my intuition. I believe solutions are a collaborative harmony, sourced from a comphresenive understanding of the needs I'm fulfilling.

Without a user, there can be no user experience. People don't always know how to encapsulate a request, but they sure know when their need is met. I'm here to help.
